Dragon Ball Z Matching Game

A throwback to your favorite childhood cartoon, DBZ Matching Game brings back some familiar faces (Z fighters and enemies alike) and allows you to match them up! But beware, the more difficult the setting, the harder the game can get. Match the wrong pair first and you'll be needing the dragon balls to bring you back to life.
